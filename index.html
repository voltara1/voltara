Welcome to VOLTARA

Unleash Your Creativity, Build, Share, and Learn Together. Connect with like-minded individuals, exchange ideas, and grow your skills in a supportive, collaborative environment.

Why learn microcontrollers?

From the rice cooker, washing machine at our home, to cars and all the way to the satellites orbiting the earth, they all have one thing in common: they all have microcontrollers at the heart of their operations. Learning microcontrollers unlocks the gateway to understand how modern technology works.

What is Voltara

VOLTARA is the ultimate online community for electronics and microcontroller enthusiasts. Whether you’re just starting your journey or an experienced hobbyist to share your knowledge, this is your space to connect, create, and collaborate.

For Everyone

Discover and Explore Projects Browse a diverse collection of user-submitted projects, from beginner-friendly circuits to advanced microcontroller builds. Use our powerful search to find projects that match your interests—whether it’s home automation, robotics, IoT, or creative lighting. Each project includes detailed circuit diagrams, build photos, source code, and build files such as the Gerber file for PCB making or .stl, or .gcode for 3D printing. Curious about how a project works? Stuck on a step? Every project page features a dedicated Q&A section where you can post questions and get answers directly from the project creator or the community.

Be a Member

Want to quickly replicate the project or add your own creativity to it? With a click of a button the build files such as the circuit diagram, code, Gerber file, .stl or gcode files will be downloaded and ready for use to use and edit for customisation.

Be a Creator

Share Your Own Creations Ready to inspire others? As a creator, you can easily upload your projects, with build photos, schematics, code, and supporting files. Respond to questions, offer tips, and become a mentor to fellow makers. Your project could be the spark that ignites someone else’s passion for electronics!

Start Your Journey Today.

Sign up now to share your projects, ask questions, or simply explore and learn. VOLTARA is where your next big idea begins!
